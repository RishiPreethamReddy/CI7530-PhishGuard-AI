# Risk Analysis

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|---|
| False positive result | Medium | Medium | Explain that the tool is advisory and requires human review |
| False negative result | Medium | High | Use cautious scoring and recommend reporting suspicious emails |
| User enters sensitive data | Medium | High | Do not store user input and include privacy warning |
| Overreliance on AI | Medium | High | Add human review warning |
| Misuse by attackers | Low | Medium | Keep the artefact defensive and avoid offensive guidance |
| Limited technical detection | Medium | Medium | State limitations clearly and suggest future improvements |
